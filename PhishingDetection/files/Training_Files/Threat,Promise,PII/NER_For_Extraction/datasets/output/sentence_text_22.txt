Subject: CMU Intern Opportunity at The Department of History To: Gregory Morris<gmorris@andrew.cmu.edu>  Students at Carnegie Mellon University!  The Department of History has some fantastic internship opportunities available, and the department welcomes applications from all majors. This opportunity is designed to provide financial assistance to students who might be facing some financial challenges, while also giving them the chance to gain some invaluable research experience.  These internships are completely remote! That means you can work from the comfort of your own home or even on-campus if you prefer.
