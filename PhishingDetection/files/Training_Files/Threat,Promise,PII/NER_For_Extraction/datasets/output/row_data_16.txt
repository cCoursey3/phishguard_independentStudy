Subject: CMU Intern Opportunity at The Department of History 
Students at Carnegie Mellon University!  

The Department of History has some fantastic internship opportunities available, and the department welcomes applications from all majors. This opportunity is designed to provide financial assistance to students who might be facing some financial challenges, while also giving them the chance to gain some invaluable research experience.  These internships are completely remote! That means you can work from the comfort of your own home or even on-campus if you prefer. The time commitment is pretty chill too, with a maximum requirement of just seven hours a week. Plus, the schedules are super flexible, so you can easily fit them around your other commitments.  If you're chosen for one of these internships, you will receive a weekly salary of Three Hundred and Fifty dollars  If this sounds like an opportunity you don't want to miss, all you need to do is reach out to Professor Jay Aronson at aronsonjjay@gmail.com [mailto:aronsonjjay@gmail.com] with your full Names, year of study, department, school email address and alternative email address (preferably Gmail) for details about the job description and any additional application requirements.  Oh, and one more thing: these positions are filled on a first-come, first-served basis. So don't waste any time and get that email sent ASAP
